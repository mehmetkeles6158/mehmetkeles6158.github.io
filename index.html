About Me

Hello, My Name is Mehmet. As a software engineer with a background in mathematics, statistics, and coaching a robotics team, I am passionate about learning new technologies and finding creative & effective solutions to daily coding challenges. As a former mathematics teacher, working with numbers and mathematical algorithms helped me in starting a new chapter in my life to coach a robotics team and learning programming languages. This passion and persistence carried me through my career development towards becoming a software developer. Problem solving, critical thinking and determination allow me to write efficient & effective code to implement and launch innovative projects & software. I leverage my technical skills and expand my knowledge to find creative solutions and make code come to life to achieve a goal. By the way, check out my my experience.

My Experience

I've started off my career as Honors Geometry Teacher in 2013. Since then, I have been a high school math teacher mostly teaching Algebra-I and Geometry classes. Besides my teaching role, I was head of math department and school testing coordinator. I was also FTC robotics coach who works with students to build and program a robot to achieve certain tasks. Teaching by itself is great thing specially teaching math is awesome. The way of math' algorithms is almost the same with the way of programming languages. This makes me so surprised and got interested to learn programming and to be a software engineer. I am currently working as an associate software engineer at Northwestern Mutual. Here is the list of my experiences.

  • Feb 2022 - Present, Associate Software Engineer at Northwestern Mutual
  • Sep 2021 - Jan 2022, Full-stack Apprentice at Actualize
  • May 2021 - Aug 2021, Java-Apprentice at CodingNomads
  • 2013-2021: Math Teacher, Head of Math Department, Testing Coordinator and Robotics Coach at HSA

My Projects

Walker on the Rails: Personally built a web app for step-tracking. App recommends the optimal number of steps per day for each user and records daily, weekly, and monthly steps to compare activity over time. The app also suggests three closest national parks to the user with parks' info by integrating NPS API. Built in Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Vue.js and Bootstrap.
